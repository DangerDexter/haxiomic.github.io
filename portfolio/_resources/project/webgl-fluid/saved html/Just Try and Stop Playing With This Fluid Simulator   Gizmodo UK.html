Just Try and Stop Playing With This Fluid Simulator

By Andrew Liszewski on 19 Nov 2014 at 6:30PM 19 Nov 2014

If you had any hope you'd actually be productive today, you'll want to avoid George Corney's magical browser-based particle fluid simulator at all costs.

You can customise the density of the particles or the quality of the simulation if it's bringing your device to a crawl, but otherwise the default settings are more than adequate to put on one amazing show with every click. [GitHub - George Corney via Skunk Bear]
