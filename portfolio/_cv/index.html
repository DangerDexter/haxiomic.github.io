Pushing past the limits of light-based microscopy
Apr/14

... Sheffield, Ashley Cadby ...
Trying to break the physical limits on light-based microscopy - Matlab: - A large collection of light emitting molecules - fluorophores - are attached to a target and triggered to blink. A series of high-speed images of the target are then taken, resulting in lots of pictures of single blurry blobs. - The centers of the blobs are individually estimated, and when collated, create a picture of the target of higher resolution than would be usually allowed by the diffraction limit - In practice, the target/microscope drifts and wobbles due to the scale of things - My job was to try and correct for the drift, given only a series of sparse point clouds
